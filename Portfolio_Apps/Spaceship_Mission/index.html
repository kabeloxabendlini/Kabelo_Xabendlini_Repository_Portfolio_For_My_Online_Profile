Chapter 1 — The Call

You are the captain of a spaceship named "The Flying Hippo." One day, you're tuning up your ship's engines when you get an urgent message on your space phone:

"Captain, one of our Mars robots is sick. We need you to go to Mars immediately and retrieve it so that we can fix it and download the results of its important experiments."

You remember that you're supposed to go to a meeting of the Space Scouts tonight, and you were really looking forward to it. But the other Space Scouts would understand — this mission is critical.

Mars, the "Red Planet," its reddish hue due to iron oxide (rust) on its surface, has captivated civilizations for millennia.

What do you do?

No problem! Come back when you're ready, Captain.

Chapter 2 — A Stowaway

Great choice! Mars is lovely this time of year — about 260 days from now!

You pack your things and take off. Launch goes smoothly and you sit back, put on your headphones, and prepare for a relaxing trip.

A week later, things are going great. Just then, you hear a strange sound.

"meow."

It sounds like Gizmo, your cat! She must have snuck aboard while you were packing!

You're happy to have the company, but you didn't pack enough food for you both, and there certainly isn't a grocery store on the way to Mars.

Risk running out of food on the return trip, or turn around now?

The End

You decide to stay home. They'll get someone else to fly to Mars.

Besides, you heard there's going to be free pizza at the Space Scouts meeting! 🍕

Please answer "Go to Mars" or "Stay Home".

Chapter 3 — A Discovery

You decide to risk it. The journey is long, and you give sweet Gizmo half your food, knowing you may not survive the return trip — but the Flying Hippo can carry the robot home without you, and at least the important data (and Gizmo) will be safe.

When you arrive at Mars, you quickly locate the sick robot. But you also discover something else: a large ice chest full of delicious sandwiches! The last repair crew must have left them here!

You carefully unwrap one. Peanut butter! Your favourite! Gizmo's favourite too! 🥜

You pack up the sandwiches and the sick robot and begin the trip home, knowing there's now more than enough food for both of you.

Mission accomplished, Captain.

The End

You turn the Flying Hippo around and head home.

When you arrive at the spaceport, your boss is standing outside.

Steam comes out of his mechanical head as he tells you there are always plenty of sandwiches on Mars — you would have been fine if you had completed your mission.

He fires you. You pick up Gizmo and start walking home.

Oh well — at least you still have Gizmo. 🐱

Please answer "Risk It" or "Go Home".
